Find a line including specified current_pos.

Parameters
textOriginal text to find a line from.
text_length_byteByte length of the original text.
current_posCurrent position taking 0 ~ (text_length - 1).
line_terminatorCharacter to terminate a line.
linePointer of start of the current line will be output.
line_length_byteByte length of current line (not including line terminator) will be output.
\n aaaaaaaaaaa \n bbbbbbbbbbbbb \n cccccccccc \n
                  ^           ^  ^
                 (1)         (2)(3)

If current_pos is at between (1) and (2), line will point at (1). If current_pos is at (3), line will also point at (1).

\n ddddddddd \n eeeeeeeeeee \0
                ^            ^
               (1)          (2)

Consider null character. If current_pos is at between (1) and (2), line will point at (1).

Find next line of specified current_pos.

Parameters
textOriginal text to find a line from.
text_length_byteByte length of the original text.
current_posCurrent position taking 0 ~ (text_length - 1).
line_terminatorCharacter to terminate a line.
linePointer of start of the next line will be output.
line_length_byteByte length of next line (not including line terminator) will be output.
Returns
false if current_pos is at the last line. true otherwise.
\n aaaaaaaaaaa \n bbbbbbbbbbbbb \n  cccccccccc \0
                  ^           ^  ^  ^
                 (1)         (2)(3)(4)

If current_pos is at between (1) and (2), next line will point at (4). If current_pos is at (3), next line will also point at (4). If curretn_pos is at (4), next line does not exist. Return false.

\n ddddddddd \n eeeeeeeeeee \n \0
                ^            ^
               (1)          (2)

Consider null character just after line terminator. If current_pos is at between (1) and (2), next line will be "".
